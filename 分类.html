茶 叶 分 类

    China is the hometown of tea trees, and China is the country with the earliest and most wild tea trees.
    According to the production methods, Chinese tea is divided into green tea, black tea, green tea, black tea, white tea and yellow tea, and many varieties are included in all kinds of tea
    China's tea industry was first developed in Bashu, then spread to the East and south one by one, and finally spread all over the country. In the Tang Dynasty, it was spread to Japan and Korea and introduced by the west after the 16th century.

    中国是茶叶的故乡，有着悠久的种茶历史，严格的敬茶礼节，奇特的饮茶风俗。茶礼有缘，古已有之,是中国汉族最早重情好客的传统美德与礼节。直到21世纪，宾客至家，总要沏上一杯香茗。喜庆活动，也喜用茶点招待。所谓君子之交淡如水，也是指清香宜人的茶水。
